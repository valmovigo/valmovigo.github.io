A Practice of Augmented Painting

Perceptrum's works investigate Augmented Painting as a third state of painting, situated between the contemplative image and the immersive environment. Through responsive surfaces, sound, touch, and computational processes, our works transform painting into a relational medium that listens, remembers, and responds. Each project represents a distinct manifestation of this ongoing artistic direction.

Sounding Canvas

A series of relational paintings that transform the canvas into a responsive surface where touch, sound, and visual form converge. Through telematic interaction and embodied perception, Sounding Canvas explores the social and semantic dimensions of Augmented Painting.

Sounding Stage

An extension of Augmented Painting into performative space. Sounding Stage investigates how responsive environments, spatialized sound, and embodied interaction can reshape the relationship between audience, performer, and architectural space.
