Featured Project: Sounding Canvas

Sounding Canvas is our seminal work that translates real-time physical data—such as ambient light, atmospheric pressure, and sub-atomic particle traces—into a dynamic auditory and visual experience.

  • **Technology:** Custom Python scripts, Arduino, and Tone.js for web deployment.
  • **Exhibited:** Venice Biennale, 2024 (Mock Content).
  • **Concept:** The work challenges the spectator's perception of unseen environmental forces.

Our Mission

Perceptrum Duo operates at the nexus of artistic expression and scientific rigor. We believe that technology is not just a tool for creation, but a language through which the hidden structures of our reality can be translated into visceral, shared human experiences. Our work seeks to elevate the subtle physics of the world into powerful, public dialogues.
